About Me

Born in the large city of Hyderabad, India, I spent four years there until I moved to Dubai; it wasn't until another four years later in 2002 that I moved to the United States for the first time. Settling in Bellevue, WA, I had the fortune of having my younger brother introduced to the family in 2004. Our family moved out to Woodinville a year later, where I graduated from Woodinville High school in 2012. In 2016, I graduated from the University of Washington with a BS in Biochemistry with an intent on pursuing medical school.

In my spare time, I love to stay active by committing to the gym four times a week. I consider myself a die-hard musician; I've been a pianist since the age of 6, and a guitarist since 13 (ask me about music - anything between Chopin and The Beatles!).
